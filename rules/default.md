# Default Community Rules

## General Guidelines

1. **Be Respectful**
   - Treat all participants with dignity and respect
   - No personal attacks or insults
   - Listen actively when others are speaking

2. **No Hate Speech**
   - Zero tolerance for discrimination based on race, gender, religion, nationality, disability, sexual orientation, or age
   - No harassment or bullying
   - No threats of violence

3. **Stay On Topic**
   - Keep discussions relevant to the conversation
   - Avoid excessive off-topic tangents
   - Respect the purpose of the room

4. **Constructive Communication**
   - Focus on ideas, not personalities
   - Provide constructive feedback
   - Acknowledge different perspectives

5. **Privacy and Safety**
   - Do not share personal information of others
   - Respect confidentiality when requested
   - Report any concerning behavior

## Enforcement

Violations of these rules may result in:
- Warning from the moderator
- Temporary muting
- Removal from the conversation
- Permanent ban for severe violations
